Access Control System

Biometric Authentication

Access Control System is a security technique that restricts access to physical or digital resources to authorized users only.

Key Features:

  • Authentication – Verifies identity (e.g., username & password)
  • Authorization – Grants or denies access based on permissions
  • Access Enforcement – Applies access rules in real time
  • Audit and Logging – Tracks who accessed what and when

Video Door Phone

A Video Door Phone is an intercom system with a camera, microphone, and speaker installed at the entrance, and a screen or app-based interface inside. It enables two-way audio and video communication between residents and visitors.

Applications of Video Door Phones

  • Residential Homes, villas, and apartments for personal security. Gated communities for visitor verification. Elderly-friendly systems with simple interfaces.
  • Commercial Offices to screen visitors or deliveries. Warehouses or factories for gate control and staff safety.
  • Hospitality Hotels and serviced apartments for secure guest interaction.
  • Institutional Schools, hospitals, and government buildings for controlled entry.
